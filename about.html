DESCRIPTION

Gem Jumpers is a fast-paced multiplayer game where two adorable blobs compete to collect as many gems as possible within a limited time frame. Players control their respective blobs, navigating them through a vibrant and dynamic map. The player who gathers the most gems emerges as the victor!

CONTROLS

HOW TO PLAY

1. Assign the Controls: Player 1 controls their blob using the keys A (left), W (up), and D (right), while Player 2 controls their blob using the arrow keys (up, left, and right).

2. Navigate the Map: Each player's blob will appear on the map once the game begins. Use the assigned controls to maneuver your blob around the terrain.

3. Collect Gems: Gems will appear on the map. Move your blob over to collect it. Each gem collected adds to your score.

4. Race Against Time: The game is timed, so collect as many gems as possible before the clock runs out. Keep an eye on the timer to gauge how much time you have left to gather gems.

5. Compete for Victory: After the time limit expires, the game ends, and the scores are tallied. The player with the highest gem count wins the round.

6. Play Again: Want a rematch? Start a new game and strive to outshine your opponent in gem-collecting prowess!

Gem Jumpers is a thrilling competition that tests your speed, agility, and strategic thinking. Are you ready to dash around the map and claim victory as the ultimate gem collector?
